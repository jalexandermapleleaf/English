📚 Study Materials & Vocabulary Practice

🔮 Predict (预测)

Definition: To guess what will happen next based on text clues and background knowledge.

Example: I predict it will rain because the clouds are dark.

🔗 Connection (联系)

Definition: A link between text and self, text, or world.

Example: This reminds me of my vacation (T-S).

✅ Fact (事实)

Definition: Something that can be proven true or false.

Example: Water boils at 100°C.

💭 Opinion (观点)

Definition: A personal belief or feeling that cannot be proven.

Example: This is the best movie ever.

🚩 Bias (偏见)

Definition: Unfair preference for one side; unbalanced view.

Example: Only showing bad news about one country.

📊 Evidence (证据)

Definition: Facts, quotes, or details that prove your idea.

Example: The text says, "He shouted angrily."
