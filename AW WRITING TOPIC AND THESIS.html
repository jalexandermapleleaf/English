Friday: Thesis Statements 周五：论点陈述

A thesis statement is the most important sentence in your essay. It states your main argument and tells the reader what your paper is about. There are three types: expository, argument, and analytical. 论点是你文章中最重要的句子。它陈述你的主要论点，并告诉读者你的论文内容。共有三种类型：说明型、论证型和分析型。

✍️ Thesis Statement Builder 论点陈述构建器

Build a strong thesis statement using your topic from Thursday. A good thesis is debatable, specific, and supportable with evidence. 用周四的主题构建有力的论点。好的论点可以辩论、具体，并且可以用证据支持。
