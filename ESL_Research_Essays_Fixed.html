🔍Research & Sources | 研究与资料来源
Primary sources give firsthand information. They come from people who saw or made something. Examples: diaries, photos, interviews, original data.
一手资料提供第一手信息。它们来自亲眼见证或创造某事的人。例如：日记、照片、采访、原始数据。
Secondary sources analyze or explain primary sources. They come from people who study the topic later. Examples: textbooks, articles, reviews.
二手资料分析或解释一手资料。它们来自后来研究该主题的人。例如：教科书、文章、评论。
🔑 The Credible Source Test | 可信来源测试
✓ Who wrote it? Are they an expert? | 谁写的？他们是专家吗？
✓ When was it published? Is it current? | 何时出版？是否最新？
✓ Where did it come from? (.edu, .gov, journals) | 来源何处？
✓ Why was it made? Is it objective or biased? | 制作目的是什么？

📝Essay Structure | 文章结构
Introduction (Paragraph 1): Hook the reader. Give background. End with your thesis statement (main argument).
引言（第一段）：吸引读者。提供背景。以论点陈述（主要论证）结束。
Body Paragraphs (2-4): Each paragraph needs: Topic sentence (main idea), Evidence (quotes/facts), Analysis (explain how evidence proves your point).
正文段落（2-4段）：每段需要：主题句（主要观点），证据（引用/事实），分析（解释证据如何证明你的观点）。
Conclusion (Final paragraph): Restate thesis in new words. Summarize main points. End with final thought (why it matters).
结论（最后一段）：用新的话重述论点。总结要点。以最终思考结束（为什么重要）。
🔑 The MEAT Rule | MEAT法则
Main Idea | 主要观点
Evidence | 证据
Analysis | 分析
Transition | 过渡
