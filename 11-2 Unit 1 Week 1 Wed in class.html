Welcome! 欢迎!

Today you will practice 3 reading strategies:

今天你将练习三种阅读策略：

🎯 1. Inference (推断)

What is happening NOW? Use evidence + background knowledge.

"Because [evidence] and I know [background knowledge], I infer [conclusion]."

因为___，而且我知道___，所以我推断___。

🔗 2. Text Connections (文本联系)

  • Text-to-Self: Connect to YOUR life
  • Text-to-Text: Connect to another book/movie/song
  • Text-to-World: Connect to real world events

文本与自我 | 文本与文本 | 文本与世界

⚖️ 3. Fact vs. Opinion (事实 vs. 观点)

  • Fact: Can be proven with data/research ✅
  • Opinion: Personal belief or feeling 💭

事实 = 可以被证明 | 观点 = 个人感受

Practice 1: Inference (推断练习 1)

Read the passage and make an inference:

阅读短文并进行推断：

Otto's Candy: Otto reached into his backpack and felt the wrapper. He smiled and looked around the room. When the teacher turned to write on the board, Otto quickly put something in his mouth.

Frame: "Because [what happened in text] and I know [my experience/knowledge], I infer [what is happening]."

Your inference:

💡 Hint: Think about when people hide food and eat it secretly. What does "quickly put something in his mouth" suggest?

Practice 2: Inference (推断练习 2)

Read the passage and make an inference:

阅读短文并进行推断：

Shawn's Test: Shawn stared at the paper on his desk. His hands were shaking. He looked at the clock, then back at the paper. He picked up his pen, put it down, and sighed deeply.

Frame: "Because [evidence] and I know [background knowledge], I infer [conclusion]."

Your inference:

💡 Hint: Think about how you feel during a difficult test. What do shaking hands and sighing tell you about Shawn's emotional state?
