📚 Unit 1 | Week 1 | 15-Minute Module

Reading Superpowers

Master Prediction & Visualization
我能根据文章线索预测接下来发生什么。我能通过作者的文字在脑海中创建画面。
I can predict what happens next using clues. I can visualize scenes from the author's words.

🎯 Today's Mission / 今日任务

You will practice 3 prediction exercises and 2 visualization exercises with different texts. By the end, you'll create a results certificate to submit.

⏱️ Time: 15 minutes | 3 Practice Rounds
🔮

PREDICTION (预测) — 3 Rounds

A guess about what will happen NEXT — based on text clues + your background knowledge.

Frame: "Because [text clue] and I know [background knowledge], I predict [what happens next]."
🎨

VISUALIZATION (可视化) — 2 Rounds

Creating a MENTAL PICTURE using the 5 senses: Sight, Sound, Smell, Touch, Taste.

5 Senses Chart: 👁️ Sight | 👂 Sound | 👃 Smell | ✋ Touch | 👅 Taste
🔮 Prediction Practice 1 of 3

Round 1: The Storm

Text: Holes by Louis Sachar
📖 Passage 1/5
"There was a change in the weather. For the worse. The air became unbearably humid. Stanley was drenched in sweat... A loud boom of thunder echoed across the empty lake. Dark clouds were rolling in from the west, covering the sun. The temperature dropped suddenly, and Stanley felt a drop of rain hit his shoulder." — Holes by Louis Sachar
Sentence Frame / 句子框架:
"Because [text clue] and I know [background knowledge], I predict [what happens next]."
💡 Clues to look for: thunder, dark clouds, humidity, temperature drop
💡 Quote specific words: "loud boom of thunder," "dark clouds," etc.
💡 What do you already know about thunderstorms?
🔮 Prediction Practice 2 of 3

Round 2: The Raft

New text, same strategy
📖 Passage 2/5
🔄 Quick Review

Remember: Good predictions combine TEXT CLUES + YOUR KNOWLEDGE.

"Jarod clung to the wooden raft as the river current grew stronger. The water, which had been calm just minutes ago, was now churning with white foam. Up ahead, he could hear a deep roaring sound that grew louder with every second. The river narrowed between two steep rock walls, and the sky above seemed to disappear into mist." — Adventure Story Excerpt
Sentence Frame: "Because [text clue] and I know [background knowledge], I predict [what happens next]."
💡 Clues: "white foam," "roaring sound," "river narrowed," "mist"
🔮 Prediction Practice 3 of 3

Round 3: The Fox & The Rabbit

Final prediction challenge
📖 Passage 3/5
✅ Checkpoint: You're halfway there!

2 predictions done, 1 more prediction + 2 visualizations to go.

"Mr. Fox crept silently through the tall grass, his eyes fixed on Mr. Rabbit who was peacefully eating carrots in the garden. The rabbit's ears twitched suddenly. He froze, nose in the air, then quickly turned toward the bushes. Mr. Fox's mouth watered as he crouched lower, his muscles tensing for the chase. But just then, the garden gate squeaked loudly as Mrs. Rabbit stepped outside with a basket." — Animal Adventure Tale
Sentence Frame: "Because [text clue] and I know [background knowledge], I predict [what happens next]."
💡 Consider: What does the rabbit notice? What does Mrs. Rabbit's arrival change?
🎨 Visualization Practice 1 of 2

Round 4: The Barn

Text: Charlotte's Web by E.B. White
📖 Passage 4/5

🎨 Strategy Switch: Visualization

Now we switch from predicting the future to picturing the present. Use your 5 senses!

"The barn was very large. It was very old. It smelled of hay and it smelled of manure. It smelled of the perspiration of tired horses and the wonderful sweet breath of patient cows. It often had a sort of peaceful smell — as though nothing bad could happen ever again in the world. It smelled of grain and of harness dressing and of axle grease and of rubber boots and of the afternoon sun on the wooden manger." — Charlotte's Web by E.B. White

Fill in at least 3 senses: / 填写至少三种感官：

👁️ Sight (视觉)
👂 Sound (听觉)
👃 Smell (嗅觉)
✋ Touch (触觉)
💡 Combine the senses into one vivid description
🎨 Visualization Practice 2 of 2

Round 5: Inside the Peach

Text: James and the Giant Peach by Roald Dahl
📖 Passage 5/5
🎯 Final Practice Round!

Last chance to sharpen your visualization skills before your results certificate.

"James discovered a secret room inside the giant peach stone. The walls were soft and cushiony, like the flesh of a ripe fruit, but firmer. A warm golden light seemed to glow from everywhere and nowhere at once. The air was thick with the sweet smell of ripe peach juice, and James could feel the gentle swaying motion of the peach rolling through the air. Strange shadows moved across the curved walls — the silhouettes of his new insect friends." — James and the Giant Peach by Roald Dahl

Fill in at least 3 senses: / 填写至少三种感官：

👁️ Sight (视觉)
👂 Sound (听觉)
👃 Smell (嗅觉)
✋ Touch/Movement (触觉/动觉)
