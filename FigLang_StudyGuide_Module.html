📖 Literary Text — How to Approach the Questions
文学文本 · 如何处理各类题目

What is a Literary Text? 什么是文学文本？

A literary text is a creative, imaginative story — a short story, novel excerpt, or narrative. It has characters, a setting, a plot, and a theme. On this exam, the literary text will be a short story that uses figurative language.

文学文本是有创意的、有想象力的故事——短篇小说、小说节选或叙事文。它有人物、场景、情节和主题。在这次考试中，文学文本将是一篇使用比喻语言的短篇故事。

📋 The 6-Question Pattern for Literary Text

  • Q1 (Recall): A directly stated fact — find it in the text. 直接在文中找答案。
  • Q2 (Recall): "According to the passage…" — exact detail. 文中的具体细节。
  • Q3 (Inference): "Why does…" — read between the lines. 推断角色的动机。
  • Q4 (Inference): "What can we infer…" — use clues to draw a conclusion. 用线索得出结论。
  • Q5 (Author's Craft): Identify a literary device — metaphor, simile, personification, etc. 识别修辞手法。
  • Q6 (Vocabulary): "What does [word] most likely mean?" — use context. 用上下文猜测词义。
Q1–2: Recall 回忆

The answer is IN the text. Read carefully — don't guess.

Q3–4: Inference 推断

The answer is SUGGESTED by the text. Use clues.

Q5: Author's Craft 写作技巧

Name the device. Think about WHY the author used it.

Q6: Vocabulary 词汇

Reread the sentence. Replace the word. Which option fits best?

🎯 How to Approach Literary Text Questions

  • Read the full story first before looking at any questions. 先读完整篇文章再看问题。
  • For Recall (Q1–2): Go back and find the exact line. Don't rely on memory. 回到文中找到确切句子。
  • For Inference (Q3–4): Ask — what does the character's action/words reveal about them? 角色的行为说明了什么？
  • For Author's Craft (Q5): Does it use "like" or "as"? → simile. No "like/as" but compares two things? → metaphor. 有没有"like/as"？
  • For Vocabulary (Q6): Cover the word, read the sentence, think of your own word — then match. 盖住单词，用自己的词替换。
  • Watch for traps: Antonym traps (opposite meaning), too literal, too extreme. 注意反义词陷阱、过于字面、过于极端的选项。

What is an Informational Text? 什么是信息文本？

An informational text (also called non-fiction or expository text) presents facts, explains ideas, or provides information about a topic. On this exam, A2 was an infographic — a visual information poster. Your questions asked about what the infographic stated, implied, and was designed to do.

信息文本（也称为非虚构或说明性文本）呈现事实、解释想法或提供有关某个主题的信息。在本次考试中，A2是一张信息图——一张视觉信息海报。问题询问信息图陈述了什么、暗示了什么以及其设计目的。

📋 The 6-Question Pattern for Informational Text

  • Q7 (Recall): What does the text DIRECTLY state? 文本直接陈述了什么？
  • Q8 (Recall): Which example / detail is mentioned? 提到了哪个例子/细节？
  • Q9 (Inference): WHY does a fact lead to a conclusion? 为什么某个事实导致某个结论？
  • Q10 (Inference): What is the DIFFERENCE between two concepts? 两个概念的区别是什么？
  • Q11 (Author's Craft): What is the PURPOSE of this text? 这篇文章的目的是什么？
  • Q12 (Vocabulary): What does [term] mean in this context? 这个词在此处的含义是什么？
Q7–8: Recall 回忆

Scan back to find the fact. It is stated clearly.

Q9–10: Inference 推断

Connect two facts. The answer is suggested, not stated.

Q11: Purpose 目的

Inform / Persuade / Entertain / Instruct — pick the best one.

Q12: Vocabulary 词汇

Use the surrounding words to figure out the meaning.

🎯 How to Approach Informational Text Questions

  • For Recall (Q7–8): The answer is written in the text. Find the section and re-read carefully. 答案就在文中，找到相关部分仔细重读。
  • For Inference (Q9–10): Ask — "If the text says X, what must also be true?" 如果文章说X，那么什么也一定是真的？
  • For Purpose (Q11): Inform = gives facts. Persuade = wants you to agree. Entertain = funny/engaging. Instruct = how-to. 告知=给出事实。说服=希望你同意。娱乐=有趣。指导=方法步骤。
  • For Vocabulary (Q12): Read the FULL sentence around the word — what feeling/meaning does the sentence need? 读包含该词的完整句子——句子需要什么感觉/含义？
  • Distractor watch: "Too narrow" (only one detail when the answer is bigger), "too extreme" (always/never), "off-topic." 注意"太窄"、"太极端"和"偏离主题"的干扰项。

🎭 Poem — How to Approach the Questions
诗歌 · 如何处理各类题目

What makes poetry different? 诗歌有什么不同？

Poetry uses compressed language — every word counts. Poets use figurative language (metaphor, simile, personification), tone, and imagery to communicate ideas and feelings. You don't need to understand every line — focus on the overall feeling and the key images.

诗歌使用"压缩的语言"——每个词都很重要。诗人用比喻语言（隐喻、明喻、拟人）、语气和意象来传达思想和感情。你不需要理解每一行——专注于整体感觉和关键意象。

📋 The 6-Question Pattern for Poem

  • Q13 (Recall): What does the poem say about [subject]? 诗歌对[主题]说了什么？
  • Q14 (Recall): What is compared to what? 什么与什么相比较？
  • Q15 (Inference): What does the speaker MEAN by this image/line? 诗人通过这个意象/诗行想表达什么？
  • Q16 (Inference): What is the CENTRAL THEME? 中心主题是什么？
  • Q17 (Author's Craft): What is the TONE? 语气是什么？
  • Q18 (Vocabulary): What does [word/phrase] suggest in this context? 这个词/短语在此处暗示什么？
Q13–14: Recall 回忆

Find the comparison or image in the poem. It is there.

Q15–16: Inference 推断

What feeling / idea is behind the image? Go deeper.

Q17: Tone 语气

What FEELING does the poet have? Choose the best word.

Q18: Suggestion 暗示

"Suggest" = what does the image/word imply? Not literal.

🎯 How to Approach Poem Questions

  • Read the poem TWICE — first for feeling, second for meaning. 读两遍——第一遍感受，第二遍理解。
  • For Recall (Q13–14): Find the exact stanza/line that answers the question. 找到回答问题的确切诗节/诗行。
  • For Inference (Q15–16): Ask — "If the poet compares X to Y, what are they saying about X?" 如果诗人将X比作Y，他们在说X的什么？
  • For Tone (Q17): Common tones: reflective (思考的), melancholic (忧郁的), hopeful (充满希望的), joyful (欢乐的), bitter (痛苦的). 常见语气词汇。
  • For "Suggest" (Q18): The question is NOT asking what literally happens — it asks what the image MEANS emotionally or symbolically. 问题不是问字面上发生了什么，而是意象的情感或象征意义。
  • Trap: Literal reading. "Rain fell like apologies" does NOT mean it was actually raining apologies. 陷阱：字面理解。比喻不是字面意思。
