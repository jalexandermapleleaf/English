Central Idea(s) | 中心思想

Non-fiction | 非虚构文本
Core Concept | 核心概念

What is a Central Idea? | 什么是中心思想？

A central idea is the most important point an author wants to communicate about a topic in a non-fiction text. It is NOT just the topic — it is what the author says about the topic.

中心思想是作者在非虚构文本中最想传达的关于某个主题的最重要观点。它不仅仅是主题——而是作者对主题说了什么。
🔑 Topic vs. Central Idea: Topic = "exercise." Central Idea = "Exercise helps students perform better in school." The central idea is a full sentence that makes a claim.
主题 = "锻炼"。中心思想 = "锻炼帮助学生在学校表现更好。"中心思想是一个完整的句子，表达一个主张。
One vs. Multiple Central Ideas | 一个还是多个中心思想？

How to decide | 如何判断

A passage has ONE central idea when all details/examples work together to prove a single main point. A passage has MULTIPLE central ideas when the author devotes equal attention to two or more points that do not support each other — they are parallel and independent.

当所有细节/例子共同证明一个主要观点时，文章有一个中心思想。当作者对两个或更多相互不支持的观点给予同等关注时，文章有多个中心思想——它们是并列且独立的。
🔑 The Test: Ask "Does idea A support idea B, or prove the same thing?" If YES → one central idea with supporting details. If NO (they are equal and separate) → multiple central ideas.
问："观点A是否支持观点B，或证明相同的事情？"如果是→一个中心思想加支持细节。如果否（它们是同等且独立的）→多个中心思想。

Theme | 主题

Fiction & Literature | 小说与文学
Core Concept | 核心概念

What is Theme? | 什么是主题？

A theme is the life lesson, message, or insight about human nature that an author communicates through a literary/fictional text. Theme is NOT a topic or a summary — it is a statement about life that applies beyond the story.

主题是作者通过文学/虚构文本传达的人生教训、信息或对人性的洞察。主题不是话题或摘要——它是关于生活的陈述，适用于故事之外。
🔑 Topic vs. Theme: Topic = "friendship." Theme = "True friendship requires honesty, even when it is painful." Always write theme as a complete sentence — a universal truth.
话题 = "友谊"。主题 = "真正的友谊需要诚实，即使诚实是痛苦的。"始终将主题写成完整的句子——一个普遍真理。
How to Find Theme | 如何找到主题

The TIPT Method | TIPT方法

Topic → Identify what the character learns → Phrase it as a universal statement → Test: Does it apply to real life?

T话题 → I识别角色学到了什么 → P将其表述为普遍陈述 → T测试：它适用于现实生活吗？
🔑 Common Traps: "The story is about a boy who learns to be brave" = summary, not theme. "Courage grows when we face our fears" = theme ✓
"这个故事讲述了一个学会勇敢的男孩"=摘要，不是主题。"当我们面对恐惧时，勇气就会增长"=主题✓
