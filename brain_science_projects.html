Case Study 1: Stanley at Camp Green Lake

Your Task: Create 8-10 slides with detailed speaker notes analyzing Stanley's psychological and behavioral changes. Explain your analysis to Mr. Alexander in person.

Required Slides:

Slide 1: Title Slide
  • Title, your name, date, image
  • Speaker Notes: Introduce yourself and topic
Slide 2: Case Background
  • Stanley before camp, his conviction, Camp Green Lake environment
  • Speaker Notes: Explain Stanley's situation in detail
Slide 3: Research Question
  • Display research question in large text
  • Speaker Notes: Explain why this question matters
Slides 4-5: Brain Science (2 slides)
  • Slide 4: Prefrontal cortex & decision-making (with visual)
  • Slide 5: Stress response & neuroplasticity (with visual)
  • Speaker Notes: Define terms, explain concepts, cite The Teenage Brain
Slides 6-7: Behavioral Analysis (2 slides)
  • Slide 6: Stanley's initial behaviors (with examples from Holes)
  • Slide 7: Changes over time & friendship with Zero
  • Speaker Notes: Describe behaviors, connect to brain science
Slide 8: Conclusions
  • Answer research question, key findings, protective factors
  • Speaker Notes: Summarize research revelations
Slide 9: Personal Reflection (30 points)
  • What surprised you, how research changed your thinking, how you'll apply it
  • Speaker Notes: Share genuine personal insights
Slide 10: References
  • List sources, thank you message
  • Speaker Notes: Thank audience, invite questions

Speaker Notes Tips

Speaker notes = what you will SAY
Include: key points, definitions, evidence, connections
Example: "The prefrontal cortex is responsible for decision-making. According to The Teenage Brain, this area isn't fully developed in teenagers..."
